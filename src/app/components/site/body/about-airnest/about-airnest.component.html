Our mission.
Our mission as a local business is to improve the experience travelers have while on the island, both inside our homes and throughout their visit.
Our values.
Everything we do and all the services we provide are in line with our deeply rooted values; quality, transparency and sustainability.
Transparency.
At AirNest, we are well aware that our services are only possible thanks to those willing to open their homes to others. That is why we take extra care in knowing that they are comfortable and have full visibility and control over their house during the entire process.
Quality.
All the accommodations we offer have undergone a strict selection process to ensure they are in line with the company’s rigorous quality standards.
